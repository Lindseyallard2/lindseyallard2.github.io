Medtronic Biometrics Experience

To enhance Medtronic’s sponsorship of US Bank Stadium and the Minnesota Vikings, Coffee built a multi-person biometrics sensor system in the Medtronic Club. To accomplish that task, we worked with our data scientist to place biometric sensors throughout the US Bank Stadium. We collected the data through an aggregation platform and displayed an interactive large scale image. The image was based on the overall emotion of the fans in the area, allowing the onlookers in the Medtronic Club to feel the momentum of the game.
